Welcome to The Naksha Factory

We exist to modernise the construction industry from the inside out. Working across both prime residential and commercial property sectors, our unique skill set allows us to fulfil our clients’ ambitions from conception to completion.

Specialists in bespoke and unique residential interiors, we enjoy working with like-minded people to deliver homes synonymous with care, quality and attention to detail. Building relationships based on collaboration, trust and understanding, we couple our expertise with your ambition in bringing your vision to life.

Founded in 1998 by B.L. Sharma, The Naksha Factory has come a long way. Pooling our 20 years of collective expertise and forensic eye for detail, we ensure every project is infused with the same combination of quality, consistency and comfort, resulting in spaces that are a joy to spend time in.
